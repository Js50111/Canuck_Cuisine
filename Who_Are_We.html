Who Are We?

We are a dedicated team of Canadian food truck owners who are committed to serving our amazing Canadian food to all of our wonderful customers. We strive for only the highest quality in our cuisine.

One of our guarantees is affordable prices so that all Canadians can enjoy our edible delights.

Our co-founder, Jujhar Seehrawent to the Chicopee Hills Ski Park in the winter for a field trip. On the way to the Ski Park, he ate some amazing poutine. The feeling he got from eating this delicious poutine, combined with talking and laughing with his friends, created a moment of happiness that he would always remember.

And we want to share that feeling of happiness with all of you!
